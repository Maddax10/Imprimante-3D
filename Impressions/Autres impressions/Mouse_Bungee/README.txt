# Summary

<b>Looking for a smart way to hold your mouse cable in place?</b>

Try this mouse bungee!

Just print both files, put the cable in between and screw the parts together.
You can use double sided tape to fix it on your desk or a M3 screw to place it on the wall.

<b>Enjoy!</b>


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EDIT:
I added a second version with higher tolerances, in case the thread of the original version is too tight for you.
"Mouse_Bungee_Body_loose" and "Mouse_Bungee_Lid_loose" should work better for printers/slicer/materials that need higher tolerances.
